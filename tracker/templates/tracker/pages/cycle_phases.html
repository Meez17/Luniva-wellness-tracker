🌸 Understanding Your Cycle Phases

Your menstrual cycle is a powerful rhythm that influences your energy, mood, and body. Learn how each phase supports your wellness journey.

🩸 Menstrual Phase (Days 1–5)

This is the start of your cycle — when bleeding occurs. Hormone levels are at their lowest, and your body is shedding the uterine lining.

  • Energy may be low — rest and gentle movement are ideal
  • Focus on nourishment, warmth, and reflection
  • Great time for journaling or quiet self-care

Tip: Embrace this phase as a time to slow down and listen to your body's needs.

🌱 Follicular Phase (Days 6–13)

Estrogen begins to rise, and your body prepares to release an egg. You may feel more energized and optimistic.

  • Ideal time for planning, creativity, and new projects
  • Try strength training, cardio, or social activities
  • Brain function and memory may feel sharper

Tip: Harness this energy to set intentions and take on challenges.

🌼 Ovulation Phase (Day 14)

This is your fertile window. Estrogen peaks and luteinizing hormone (LH) triggers the release of an egg.

  • Energy, confidence, and libido are often highest
  • Great time for communication, collaboration, and visibility
  • Skin may glow, and mood is often elevated

Tip: Use this phase to connect with others and showcase your talents.

🌙 Luteal Phase (Days 15–28)

Progesterone rises to support a potential pregnancy. If no fertilization occurs, hormone levels drop, leading to PMS symptoms.

  • Energy may dip — focus on rest, boundaries, and self-care
  • Cravings, bloating, and mood swings are common
  • Gentle exercise and calming routines are helpful

Tip: Prioritize self-compassion and listen to your body's signals during this phase.
